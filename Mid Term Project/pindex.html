Muhammad Ali Jinnah

Muhammad Ali Jinnah was a barrister, politician and the founder of Pakistan. Jinnah served as the leader of the All-India Muslim League from 1913 until the inception of Pakistan on 14 August 1947, and then as the Dominion of Pakistan's first Governor-General until his death.

Died: September 11, 1948, Karachi

Imran Khan

Imran Ahmed Khan Niazi HI PP is the 22nd and current Prime Minister of Pakistan and the chairman of the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf. Before entering politics, Khan was an international cricketer and captain of the Pakistan national cricket team, which he led to victory in the 1992 Cricket World Cup.
Read more on Wikipedia.

Born: October 5, 1952 (age 68 years), Lahore
Spouse: Bushra Bibi (m. 2018), Reham Khan (m. 2014–2015), Jemima Goldsmith (m. 1995–2004)
Education: Keble College (1972–1975), RGS Worcester, Aitchison College
Children: Sulaiman Isa Khan, Kasim Khan

Muhammad Atif Aslam is a Pakistani playback singer and actor. He has recorded numerous chart-topping songs in both Pakistan and India and is known for his vocal belting technique.
